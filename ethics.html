Course: CPRE/EE 234 - Cybersecurity Ethics
Topic: Chief Information Security Officer Risk Assessment for Iowa State University

This ethics paper explores the responsibilities and challenges of being a Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) at Iowa State University. Using the STRIDE methodology (Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, and Elevation of Privilege), the paper conducts a comprehensive risk assessment of the university's critical IT assets.
